⚡ How it works

Up and running in under an hour

No technical knowledge required. If you can use WhatsApp, you can run The MiddleMan.

1

Connect your calendar

Link your Google Calendar in one click. The MiddleMan reads your real-time availability and respects any existing events and blocks you have set.

2

Set your rules via WhatsApp

Tell your PA how you work — business hours, service duration, lead time, special instructions. Just send a message, like you would to a new hire.

3

Share your WhatsApp number

Give customers a single WhatsApp number. They message in plain English — your PA handles the rest and keeps your calendar spotless.
