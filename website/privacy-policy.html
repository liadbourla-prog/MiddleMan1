Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

MiddleMan ("we", "our", or "us") operates a WhatsApp-based personal assistant platform for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you interact with our service.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our service:

  • WhatsApp messages — content of messages you send to or receive from our service, used solely to fulfil your requests (e.g. booking appointments).
  • Phone number — your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify your session and deliver replies.
  • Calendar data — appointment details (date, time, service type) provided during booking.
  • Google Calendar access — if a business owner connects their Google Calendar, we access it only to read/write appointments on their behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process appointment bookings and send confirmations.
  • To maintain your session state during a conversation.
  • To route messages to the correct business.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

3. Data Retention

Conversation session data is retained for 30 days and then deleted. Booking records are retained for as long as the business account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Security

All data is stored in encrypted Google Cloud infrastructure (Europe region). Access to production databases is restricted to authorised personnel only. We use HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Cloud API) — message delivery. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud — infrastructure and storage. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Calendar API — calendar integration (only when explicitly authorised by the business owner).
  • Vertex AI (Google) — natural language processing to understand your requests.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
